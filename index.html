The Keys Cocktail Hour

Cocktail hour, the early-evening practice of meeting up with friends and family over a light drink and a snack. These are different opportunities to slow down from the day before heading out to dinner. Could anything make Cocktail hour in Islamorada more magical? Yes! Try having your sunset dinner at the Sand Bar literally surrounded by the spectacular Islamorada view.

Make the Perfect Rum Punch!

This traditional cocktail gives you the most refreshing taste of summer, with only a few ingredients:

  • 12 ounces dark rum
  • 12 ounces white rum
  • 3 ounces pineapple juice
  • 1 ounce orange juice, freshly squeezed
  • 1 ounce lime juice, freshly squezed
  • 1/4 ounce grenadine

Salud!
